Phase 1 — Minutes 0-5

Before the Demo Starts

Preparation and first impressions set the tone for the entire conversation.

1

Pre-Demo Checklist

Do this 10 minutes before the call starts

2

Opening the Call

Build rapport and discover pain points — first 5 minutes

SAY THIS
  • Namaste [Name] ji! Aaj ka demo dekh ke aap excited ho jayenge.
  • Mujhe bata ke pehle — aap currently WhatsApp pe kitne leads per day receive karte ho?
  • Aur unhe manually reply karne mein kitna time lagta hai?
  • Aapka biggest challenge kya hai — lead quality, follow-up, ya response time?
DON'T SHOW YET
  • Just listen and take notes on their pain points
  • Note their city and property types they deal in
  • Find out team size — 1 agent vs 10 agents changes your pitch
  • Discover if they've tried other tools before
PRO TIP
Golden Question: "Agar WhatsApp pe aane wale 100% leads ko instantly reply mile aur qualify ho jaye — bina aapke ek minute lagate — aapka business kaisa dikhega?" Let them describe their dream. Then show that you have it.
Phase 2 — Minutes 5-20

The Live Demo — Step by Step

This is the core of your pitch. Go through each step clearly and let them interact.

3

Show the Landing Page

Start with social proof — 30 seconds only

SAY THIS
  • "Pehle aapko dikhata hoon — 500+ agents already use PropBot across India."
  • "Yeh chat demo dekho — exactly aisa hi conversation aapke buyers ke saath hoga."
  • "Hindi-English mixed — exactly waisi bhasaha jisme Indian buyers comfortable hain."
SHOW THIS
  • Open index.html — let the chat animation play for 15 seconds
  • Point to the stats: 500+ agents, ₹50Cr+ deals, 10,000+ leads
  • Hover over the features section briefly
  • Move fast — don't spend more than 90 seconds here
Speed matters: Don't let them read every word. You're building curiosity. The LIVE demo on their phone will do the real convincing.
4

The Live WhatsApp Demo 🔥

Most powerful part — have them experience it themselves

SAY THIS EXACTLY
"[Name] ji, ab main aapko kuch karne ko bolunga. Apna phone uthao aur is WhatsApp number pe sirf 'Hi' bhejo: +91-XXXXX-XXXXX. Aap khud buyer ban jao aur experience karo ki aapke future customers kya dekhenge."
AS THEY CHAT
  • Stay quiet and let them read the bot responses
  • "Notice karo — Hindi mein kitni natural conversation hai"
  • "Bot ne automatically next question pooch liya — bina aapke kuch kiye"
  • "Ab budget select karo aur dekho kya hota hai"
  • Point out: "Yeh property matching real-time mein ho raha hai"
SIMULTANEOUSLY SHOW
  • Open /dashboard in your screen
  • Show the lead appearing in real-time (or refresh)
  • "Dekho — aapka lead dashboard pe aa gaya!"
  • Show the lead score updating as they progress
  • When they book a visit — show it in the visits tab
CLIMAX MOMENT
"[Name] ji — yeh sab hua without you doing ANYTHING. 3 minutes mein aapne: qualify kiya, property match kiya, site visit book kiya, aur lead dashboard pe save ho gaya. 24 ghante mein, 7 din, automatically. Yeh hai PropBot."
Emotional trigger: After the bot completes the flow, ask "Agar aapke 50 buyers kal yahi experience karein — kitne site visits aayenge?" Let them calculate it themselves. Their own number will convince them better than yours.
5

Show the Admin Dashboard

Sell the data — agents love visibility

SAY THIS
  • "Yeh aapka war room hai — sab kuch ek jagah."
  • "Hot leads — yeh woh log hain jo abhi kharidne ke liye ready hain."
  • "Dekho — score 7+ wale leads ko hum instantly agent ko notify karte hain."
  • "Revenue calculator — khud calculate karo kitna kamaoge."
CLICK THROUGH
  • Overview tab — stats, chart, recent leads
  • Click "All Leads" — show filter by Hot/Warm/Cold
  • Click "Site Visits" — show confirmed visits table
  • Click "Revenue Calculator" — enter their typical numbers
  • Show the ROI result — let the number speak

Phase 3 — Minutes 20-25

Handling Objections Like a Pro

Every objection is a buying signal. These are the most common ones and exactly what to say.

"Bahut expensive hai — ₹15,000 per month bahut zyada hai"
Response: "[Name] ji, expensive nahi — yeh ek investment hai. Calculator mein aapne khud dekha — 10 deals × ₹75,000 = ₹7.5 lakh/month. PropBot ki cost? ₹15,000. Agar sirf ek extra deal close ho — bot ka cost recover. Baaki sab profit. Aap actually expensive wala choice le rahe ho by NOT using PropBot."
"WhatsApp Business se kaam chal jaata hai — iska kya zaroorat?"
Response: "WhatsApp Business me aap manually reply karte ho — 24 ghante nahi, sirf jab aap awake hain. PropBot 3 AM ko bhi lead qualify karta hai jab aap so rahe hain. Plus lead scoring, visit booking, agent alerts, Google Sheets CRM — yeh sab WhatsApp Business nahi karta. Aap WhatsApp Business use karte hain — toh aap already understand karte ho WhatsApp ka power. Ab uss power ko 100x karo."
"Technical nahi hoon — setup karna mushkil hoga"
Response: "Aapko kuch nahi karna — hum sab karte hain. Aap sirf apni property list aur WhatsApp number dena — hum 24 ghante mein live kar dete hain. Aur agar kuch problem aaye — humara team WhatsApp pe available hai. Aapke doctor se poochho ki operation kaise karna hai? Nahi — aap unhe trust karte ho aur woh karte hain. Hum wahi karte hain aapke liye."
"Pehle kisi aur ke saath try karna chahta hoon / sochke batata hoon"
Response: "Sochna bilkul sahi hai — lekin consider karo: jab aap soch rahe ho, aapke competitors who are already using this are getting qualified leads RIGHT NOW. Real estate mein timing everything hai. Aaj se 3 din baad agar aap call karein aur bolein 'start karo' — hum immediately karenge. Lekin jo leads aaj aaye, kuch aur agent ne close kiye honge. Ek kaam karo — 7-day free trial mein shuru karo. Zero risk."
"Clients WhatsApp bot se baat karenge kya? Bohot impersonal lagta hai"
Response: "Aapne khud abhi bot se baat ki — impersonal laga? 80% buyers actually bot prefer karte hain because: no judgment, answer 24/7, no waiting, no sales pressure. Bot qualify karta hai — agent tab aata hai jab buyer READY hota hai. Yeh more personal hai — kyunki buyer khud choose karta hai kab aapse baat karni hai."
"Abhi budget nahi hai"
Response: "Samajh aa gaya. Ek baat bata do — abhi aapke leads kahan se aate hain? [They say Magicbricks/99acres] Kitna pay karte ho? [₹5,000-50,000/month typically] PropBot ₹5,000 mein shuru hota hai — Starter plan. Wo leads aapko quality nahi dete, sirf quantity. PropBot qualified leads deta hai with visit bookings. Actually aap paise BACH rahe ho switch karke."
